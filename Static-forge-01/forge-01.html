Veg Biriyani

Aromatic and flavorful rice dish with mixed vegetables and spices,

Modal title

Veg Biryani

Description

A fragrant and flavorful rice dish made with basmati rice, mixed vegetables, herbs, and aromatic Indian spices.

    Prepration

  • Prep: 20 minutes
  • Cook: 30 minutes
  • Total: 50 minutes
Servings 4 people

Ingredients

For Rice

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt to taste

For Vegetables

  • 1 cup carrots (diced)
  • 1 cup beans (chopped)
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1 potato (cubed)
  • 1 onion (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (slit)

Spices & Herbs

  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves

Other Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste
